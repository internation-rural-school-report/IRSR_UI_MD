Our Goals

To Help children in Sub-Saharan Africa lead healthier, more productive lives. Our work supports education, health, early preparation against pandemics, safety, particularly for orphans and girls, who face greater risk for violence, neglect and illness.

Our Projects

We train children and teenagers to Develop critical thinking and problem solving skills to make better choices and decisions.

IRSR mobilizes entire communities to launch and sustain life-changing programs and self-help initiatives. Thousands of children have been motivated to stay in school, avoid risky behaviors and contribute to human progress in their own villages by volunteering. Our five-year campaign, which started in 2018, seeks to provide support for one million at-risk children, helping them to enter and stay in school.

Donation

IRSR’s Surround a child sponsorship program which provides children with the resources necessary to break out of the cycle of illiteracy, poverty and dependency. With a sponsor’s $10 or more monthly contribution, a child is able to go to school and receive nutritional and health support.
